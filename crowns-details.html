If Crowns You Seek

Project Overview

"If Crowns You Seek" is an original short animation that explores themes of purpose and destiny, reflecting on the biblical narrative of kingship. This project combines elements of traditional animation and modern storytelling techniques to convey a deep and meaningful message inspired by scripture.

Inspiration & Process

The project was inspired by the concept of kingship in the Bible, particularly the stories of King Saul, David, and Jesus as the ultimate king. The animation style was influenced by a combination of classic 2D animation techniques and modern digital tools, aiming to create a visually compelling and thematically rich narrative.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Animate
  • Photoshop
  • After Effects
