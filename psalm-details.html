Psalm 91:4 Illustration

Project Overview

This illustration is a creative interpretation of Psalm 91:4, which emphasizes God's protection and faithfulness. The imagery was designed to evoke a sense of divine shelter, drawing from the metaphor of being under God's wings.

Inspiration & Process

The artwork was inspired by the comforting promise of God's protection in Psalm 91:4: "He will cover you with his feathers, and under his wings you will find refuge; his faithfulness will be your shield and rampart." The illustration process involved detailed sketching, digital painting, and a focus on capturing the symbolism of protection and care.

Tools Used

  • Procreate
  • Wacom Tablet
